ABOUT US
Who are We?

We are a mom-and-daughter duo working from a home-based setup, crocheting home accessories with love. Mom has been crocheting for years as a hobby, but we're thrilled to now put crochet out in the world for everyone to experience its warmth. A small but sufficient space, big dreams, and a million ideas is us in a nutshell!





Crochet Culture

Crochet is one of the ultimate Pakistani family traditions apart from knitting, but is also a pretty popular yarn craft all across the globe. There exists a handmade crochet alternative for nearly everything in your house – coasters, baskets, and even blankets. In this era of essential synthetics, we stand for keeping alive the traditions of motherly love and the infusing the warmth of handmade knots in households all across Pakistan.

Our Goals

Going Eco-Friendly:
As you help us grow, we can afford to intervene in yarn manufacturing processes and ensure that our materials reach us 100% eco-friendly. From there onwards, you can count on us!

Empowering Women Nationwide:
Pakistani women are super talented in crafts, and what could be better than them unlocking their potential to achieve self-sufficiency? We aim to expand the Knotches family by training and hiring underprivileged women – empowering them within their comfort zone to build a better Pakistan.
